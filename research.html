RESEARCH


Internal full-time Candidates: A candidate who wishes to work for Ph.D degree on full time (including project staff working in sponsored projects being carried out at the institute) should apply in the prescribed form on or before the due dates announced every year.


PROCEDURE


a. Applications will be invited by open advertisement in all leading news papers / local announcement for all categories of candidates mentioned in section 2.

b. No VIT entrance examination (VITREE).

c. Selected candidates will be called for a personal interview conducted at the campus where the admission is sought.

d. Final Selection will be based on the overall merit index of the candidate which will be arrived at by taking into account the candidate’s performance in the previous university examinations at Master’s level and the performance in the written test, research output and / the performance in personal interview at VIT.


ELIGIBILITY


For Ph.D. in Engineering:

1. Master’s degree in Engineering/Technology in the relevant discipline with a first class or a minimum of 60% marks/CGPA 6.5 on a 10 point scale in cases where the University/Institute does not award any class or

2. Equivalent qualifications like M.Sc. (Engineering) / M.S. [By Research.] / M.Tech [By Research].

For Ph. D in Sciences :

Master’s degree in the relevant discipline of Science with a first class (or a minimum of 60% marks/CGPA 6.5 on a 10 point scale in cases where the University/ Institute does not award any class).

For Ph. D in Languages:

Master’s degree with a minimum of 60% marks or 6.5 CGPA

For Ph. D in Management:

Master in Business Administration / CA / ICWA with a first class or a minimum of 60% marks/CGPA 6.5 on a 10 point scale in cases where the University/Institute does not award any class.

For Ph.D. in Law:

Master's Degree in law (LLM/ML) from any recognized University/Institute, in the relevant field with a minimum of 60% marks or 6.5 CGPA.

For Ph. D in Fashion Technology:

M.Tech./M.E./M.Fashion Tech/M.S.[By Research]/M.Tech[By Research] in the field of Textile/Textile Engineering/Fibre Science/Textile Chemical Processing/Fashion/Apparel/Garment with a first class or a minimum of 60% marks/CGPA 6.5 on a 10 point scale in cases where the University/Institute does not award any class.

Note: M.Sc Computer Science, M.Sc (IT), MCA and M.S.(Software Engg.) degree holders are not eligible to apply for PhD. Program.
